Cinnamon Buns


This exceptional recipe delivers bakery-quality cinnamon rolls with an incredibly tender crumb and indulgent, sticky-sweet center. Using a bread machine for the initial mixing, the dough incorporates instant vanilla pudding mix as a secret ingredient that ensures each bite stays wonderfully fluffy and never dry. The rich brown sugar and cinnamon swirl creates that coveted caramelized filling, while optional nuts and dried fruit add delightful bursts of flavor and texture. Finished with a decadent vanilla butter frosting, these homemade treats are ideal for special brunches or cozy weekend baking. This tried-and-true recipe has earned rave reviews from home bakers who praise its foolproof method and consistently delicious results.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 tablespoons water
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 4 cups bread flour
  • ½ (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons bread machine yeast
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup chopped walnuts (Optional)
  • ¼ cup raisins (Optional)
  • 1 ½ cups confectioners' sugar
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Place milk, 4 tablespoons melted butter, water, beaten egg, bread flour, pudding mix, sugar, salt, and yeast in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select Dough cycle.
  2. Remove dough from machine after the cycle is done; knead 3 to 5 minutes. Roll out to a large rectangle. Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  3. Combine brown sugar, 1/2 cup softened butter, and cinnamon in a bowl; spread over dough. Sprinkle walnuts and raisins over top.
  4. Roll dough into a log, starting with the widest end; pinch to seal seams. Cut into 1/2- to 1-inch slices; place in the prepared baking pan. Place in a draft-free space; let rise until doubled in volume.
  5. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes.
  7. Meanwhile, combine confectioners' sugar, 4 tablespoons softened butter, milk, and vanilla in a small bowl; spread over warm cinnamon rolls.
